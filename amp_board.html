Amplifier Board Design

One of our key elements of our project is our wide band digital amplifier, designed to operate at ultrasonic frequencies. Using a modern class D amplifier IC, we can deliver up to 50W of power to our ultrasonic transducer array. Our current layout was developed with through-hole parts in mind, to simplify assembly and development. A more compact layout could have been achieved if more surface mount components were used, but that would be less desirable for prototyping purposes.
