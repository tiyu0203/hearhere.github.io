Amplifier Board Design

Hear Here 2.0 is a reimagining of the original Hear Here targeted speaker concept developed by Rafi, Sam, and Misha in 2019. It uses a parametric speaker to deliver directed audio, guided by computer vision to ensure continuous audio delivery to a particular target. Our device is designed to deliver personalized audio to a single person within the scope of a room.

What are our goals? A parametric speaker is a special kind of audio device designed to deliver an audio beam much narrower than a conventional loudspeaker. Such a beam can be narrow enough to affect only a single person in a crowded room, which makes this technology perfect for targeted advertisement or content delivery.
